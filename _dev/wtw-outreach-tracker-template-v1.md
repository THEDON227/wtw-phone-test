# WTW Outreach Tracker Template v1

## 1. Purpose
This tracker is for organizing WTW outreach, follow-ups, and relationship tracking across the launch markets.

It helps Kwame keep the outreach pipeline clear and avoid losing momentum as WTW moves into partner acquisition mode.

## 2. Tracking Rules
- Track every contact
- Do not mark anyone as partner until confirmed
- Separate warm contacts from cold contacts
- Keep notes short and useful
- Track the next action clearly
