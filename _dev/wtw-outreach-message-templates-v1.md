# WTW Outreach Message Templates v1

## 1. Purpose
This is an internal outreach kit for controlled WTW partner and investor conversations.

It is designed to help Kwame communicate clearly, keep the pitch premium, and avoid overpromising while the launch network grows.

## 2. Outreach Strategy
Start with warm contacts first.

Target reachable managers and operators first.

Use big venues for vision, but focus on smaller and mid-size venues for first wins.

Keep the ask simple.

Do not overpromise.

The goal is not to sell everything at once. The goal is to open the right door for a private WTW pilot.

## 3. First Ask
Main ask:

“Would you be open to being part of a private WTW founding partner pilot?”

What we are asking for:
- point of contact
- how they want requests routed
- what they can offer Wave Pass members
- permission to send curated requests
- feedback loop after experiences
