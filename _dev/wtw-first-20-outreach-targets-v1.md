# WTW First 20 Outreach Targets v1

## 1. Purpose
This is the first internal outreach list for controlled partner conversations across WTW’s launch markets.

It is designed to help Kwame focus on the strongest early targets while keeping the outreach process organized and realistic.

## 2. Targeting Rules
- This is not a partner list
- These companies are targets only
- Do not call anyone an official partner until confirmed
- Start with reachable managers and operators first
- Big-name venues are useful for vision, but first wins may come from mid-size lounges, restaurants, promoters, and hosts

## 4. Outreach Difficulty Legend
- Easy: likely reachable with a warm intro or direct contact
- Medium: reasonable to reach with targeted outreach
- Hard: strong target but harder to secure
- Anchor / Strategic: high-value story target for WTW positioning

## 7. Market-Specific Angles

### NYC
Focus on nightlife demand, VIP/table requests, curated event discovery, and dinner-to-night routing.

### New Jersey
Focus on NYC/NJ birthday groups, waterfront dining, Hoboken/Jersey City nightlife, Atlantic City trips, bachelor/bachelorette groups, and weekend demand.

### Miami
Focus on out-of-town groups, birthdays, bachelor/bachelorette demand, dinner-to-club routing, VIP tables, Wave Pass, and post-experience feedback.

## 8. First Ask Script
Main ask:

“Would you be open to being part of a private WTW founding partner pilot?”

What to ask for:
- request routing contact
- preferred table / guest list / reservation rules
- Wave Pass member value
- permission to send curated requests
- feedback loop

## 9. What Not To Say
- guaranteed entry
- guaranteed table
- official partner
- verified partner
- instant confirmation
- fixed minimum spend
- confirmed availability without partner approval
