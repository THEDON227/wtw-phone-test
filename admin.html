Events
events
Discovery inventory
Title, venue, market, date, times, music, dress code, age, price, guest list, and VIP table flags.
Status-driven publishing model for launch, draft, and archived states.
Venues
venues
Indulge inventory
Dining rooms, rooftops, lounges, and dinner-to-nightlife spots by market.
Reservation, table, and guest list availability is tracked at the venue layer.
Reservations
reservation_requests
Concierge intake
Guest name, contact, market, venue, requested date/time, party size, occasion, and notes.
Later this can power concierge routing and partner review queues.
Ticket + Guest List
ticket_requests
Event conversion
Ticket type, quantity, total amount, plus guest list arrivals and event links.
Separated tables keep the event flow clean and easy to query.
Partners
partner_applications
Supply network
Restaurants, rooftops, lounges, promoters, and hospitality operators apply here.
Designed to route qualified high-intent guests to the right rooms.
Wave Pass
wave_pass_requests
Membership interest
Tracks early interest before login, payments, or digital card issuance exist.
Leaves room for later tiers, accounts, and QR-ready access.
